FIX.5.0SP2 Components

AllocRegulatoryTradeIDGrp [type 'Component']

The AllocRegulatoryTradeIDGrp is a repeating component within the TrdAllocGrp component used to report the source, value and relationship of multiple trade identifiers for the same trade allocation instance. This component can be used to meet regulatory trade reporting requirements where identifiers such as the Unique Swaps Identifier (USI) are required to be reported, showing the chaining of these identifiers as needed.
