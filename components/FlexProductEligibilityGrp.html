FIX.5.0SP2 Components

FlexProductEligibilityGrp [type 'Component']

The FlexProductEligibilityGrp component is used to specify whether securities within a product group or complex are eligible for creating flexible securities.

Field/Component Field Name FIXML name Req'd Comments Depr.
2560 NoFlexProductEligibilities None
1242 FlexProductEligibilityIndicator @FlexProdElig Required if NoFlexProductEligibilities(2560) > 0.
2561 FlexProductEligibilityComplex @FlexProdEligCmplx Required if NoFlexProductEligibilities(2560) > 0. Used to specify a product suite related to an eligibility indicator.
