FIX.5.0SP2 Components

StreamCommoditySettlTimeGrp [type 'Component']

The StreamCommoditySettlTimeGrp is a repeating subcomponent of the StreamCommoditySettlDayGrp component used to define the settlement time periods associated with the commodity contract.

41286 NoStreamCommoditySettlTimes

41288 StreamCommoditySettlEnd @End Required if NoStreamCommoditySettlTimes(41286) > 0.
41588 StreamCommoditySettlTimeType @Typ May be defaulted to market convention or bilaterally agreed if not specified.
