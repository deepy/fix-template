FIX.5.0SP2 Fields

LegMidPx [type '2346']

Leg Mid price/rate. For OTC swaps, this is the mid-market mark (for example, as defined by CFTC). For uncleared OTC swaps, LegMidPx(2346) and the MidPx(631) fields are mutually exclusive.
